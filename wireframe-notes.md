# SPRK Wireframe Notes

## Home Feed
The home feed uses a three-column desktop layout with a left navigation rail, central feed, and right sticky rail. The central rail begins with tabs and a featured merchandise post, then featured creators, a Cyberpunk-style feature card, a sponsored event ad, trending shorts, a design promo, and a promoted Pavilion ad. The left rail includes navigation, My SPRK items, CTA and newsletter cards, plus dynamic ad placement. The right rail contains trending topics, a dynamic ad, and upcoming events.

## Article Detail
The article detail retains the left rail and right sticky rail. The central column starts with a paid ad strip, a feature article headed by the SPRK 0025 title and image, rich text paragraphs, link styling, post reactions, then a dark embedded comment/thread section. The right rail emphasizes SPRK*Pavilion, dynamic ad zones, and trending sparks.
